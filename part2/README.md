## Report Direction

Part 2 should follow the competition rubric:

- **Descriptive:** summarize what happened with clear charts and correct aggregations.
- **Diagnostic:** explain why patterns may have happened using segment comparisons and supporting evidence.
- **Predictive:** discuss likely future patterns from seasonality, channel quality, or leading indicators.
- **Prescriptive:** propose concrete business actions supported by the data.

## Working Themes

The current report draft points toward these themes:

1. Revenue trend and seasonality.
2. Product/category/segment performance.
3. Customer and channel quality.
4. Operations, promotion, delivery, and returns.
